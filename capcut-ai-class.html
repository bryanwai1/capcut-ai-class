Cinematography

Camera Angles for Better AI Visuals

  • Eye level — natural perspective
  • Low angle — power and confidence
  • High angle — vulnerability or overview
  • Over the shoulder — conversation scenes
  • Top down — workflow or desk shots
  • Close up — emotion or detail
  • Wide shot — environment context
